Oh , well hello there!

Hello and welcome to my first ever blog! I am so excited to finally create this as I have been in the thought of it for many years now, and have finally done it! I hope you enjoy my blog about my opinions and thoughts on different books and my lifestyle. Also, did I forget to introduce myself ? Oh, I did, didnt I? Well, my name is Ume Laila, and I am a high school student who gets her head out of the clouds. Starting from reality. As someone young I have many hopes and dreams that, will not align with reality. Like I seriously hope to solve a murder mystery that somehow may have some correlation to my friends or such. Or my standards. Let's just say they are too high. Even beyond the night sky. As a reader and the sweet responsibilities that come along with it. I absolutely enjoy writing. Writing in the sense of fiction and fantasy. Writing, in my opinion, is the best way I can express my feelings at their best. If I remember quite correctly, I first started writing back when I was 8! Yes 8. Although my writing sucked, I guess you can say I developed a great passion for it. And well, then I got into reading and got myself to the point of becoming a writer.
This blog is mainly for me to express my love for reading as well as writing. I have made this blog for you and I to find the latest or the most under-rated recommendations. Varying from many genres. I hope you enjoy reading about my reviews and my opinions on books, and that you find this a safe place to express your own opinions on your favourite books.
